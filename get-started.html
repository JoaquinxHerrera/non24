Let’s Get Started

This screening helps gather the information a licensed medical provider needs to evaluate your sleep symptoms and determine whether treatment may be appropriate.
‍
What to expect
  1. You’ll start with a few quick questions to confirm basic eligibility.
  2. You’ll then complete a clinical questionnaire about your sleep and overall health.
  3. A licensed medical provider will review your submission asynchronously and determine whether treatment may be appropriate. No live appointment is required.
You can complete everything at your own pace, and most people finish the screening in about 10 minutes.

Before You Continue
By continuing, you acknowledge that:
  • Your responses will be reviewed by a licensed medical provider as part of your clinical evaluation.
